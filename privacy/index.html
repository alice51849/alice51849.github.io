Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-22 · Lumi Studio

Lumi Studio ("we", "us") builds privacy-first apps for kids and families. This policy explains how our apps and our social media tooling handle data.

1. Information we do not collect

Our apps are designed to run on-device. We do not collect, sell, or share personal data such as your name, contacts, photos, or precise location for advertising. Most processing happens locally on your iPhone.

2. Information processed

If an app offers optional features (e.g. cloud sync or analytics), only the minimum data needed is processed, and never sold. Purchases are handled by Apple; we never see your payment details.

3. Third-party platforms

We operate official accounts on platforms such as TikTok, YouTube, Threads and Pinterest to share promotional videos of our own apps. When we publish via these platforms' official APIs, we only post our own content; we do not access your personal account data.

4. Children's privacy

Our kids' apps comply with COPPA and Apple's Kids Category rules: no third-party ads, no behavioral tracking, and no collection of personal information from children.
