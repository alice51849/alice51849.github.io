How can we help?

Mochi DoneStamp for iPhone and iPad

How do I record an event?

Tap the round check button on an event card to record the current time. A brief Undo action appears at the bottom. Touch and hold the same button to choose an earlier date and time.

Why did the next reminder move?

Every rhythm restarts from the actual completion time. If you finish three days late, the next reminder starts from that new time instead of keeping an outdated schedule.

Why am I not receiving reminders?

Open the event, choose Event Settings, confirm that it has a rhythm and that Remind Me is enabled, then check iOS Settings > Notifications > Mochi DoneStamp. The app only asks for permission after you turn on a reminder.

How do I restore Lifetime Pro?

Open Settings in Mochi DoneStamp, choose Mochi DoneStamp Pro, and tap Restore Purchase. Use the same Apple Account that made the original purchase. Lifetime Pro is a non-consumable purchase, not a subscription.

How do backups work?

Lifetime Pro can create a complete JSON backup or a CSV history export from Settings. A complete backup includes event history and photos. Keep that file somewhere you trust, then use Restore Backup to merge it with current data or replace current data.
