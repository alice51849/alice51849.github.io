Privacy Policy

Effective 17 July 2026

Summary

Mochi DoneStamp does not require an account and does not include advertising, analytics, tracking, or third-party SDKs. Core use is offline. Lumi Studio does not operate a server for the app and cannot read your on-device content.

Data stored on your device

Event names, symbols, colours, rhythms, reminder settings, completion history, notes, and selected photos are stored in the app's protected App Group container so the app, widgets, notification actions, and App Intents can use the same data. A limited snapshot of up to 50 events is sent to your paired Apple Watch through Apple's WatchConnectivity service. It includes event names, symbols, colours, completion and due timing, overdue, pin and sharing status, an optional completion name, and the app language; notes and photos are not sent to the Watch. Recording an event on the Watch sends the event and occurrence identifiers and completion time back to the paired iPhone through WatchConnectivity. None of this content is sent to Lumi Studio.

Photos

Photos are optional and selected by you. Mochi DoneStamp creates a compressed private copy for the event history. It does not upload selected photos to Lumi Studio or include them in CloudKit family sharing.

Optional family sharing through iCloud

Family Sharing starts only after you choose Share with Family. Apple's private and shared CloudKit databases then process the event and occurrence identifiers; event name, symbol, colour, display order, pinned or archived status, recurrence and reminder settings, creation and update times; and completion times, source, notes, and participant identifiers and display names. Selected photos are not uploaded. Only people you invite through Apple's sharing interface can access a shared event. Lumi Studio cannot access that content. You can manage participants, stop sharing, or delete the shared event in the app. Apple's privacy policy and your iCloud settings also apply.

Purchases

Lifetime Pro purchases and restorations are processed by Apple through StoreKit. Lumi Studio does not receive or store your payment card details. Apple may process purchase information under its own privacy policy.

Notifications

Reminder notifications are scheduled locally on your device. Their "Done" and "Snooze" actions update the same local App Group data. Mochi DoneStamp does not use a remote notification server.

Artificial intelligence

Mochi DoneStamp does not use an external AI or model provider and does not send your data to a model. It also does not run a model on your content on the device.

Analytics, advertising, and tracking

Mochi DoneStamp contains no advertising or analytics SDK, does not track you across apps or websites, and does not sell personal information.

Backups, exports, and retention

Backups and JSON exports are created only when you request them and are saved to a location you choose. You control and delete those files. On-device content remains until you edit or delete it, reset the app, or remove it through iOS. The paired Apple Watch keeps its latest limited event snapshot until a newer snapshot replaces it or the Watch app is removed. Shared content remains in CloudKit until you stop sharing or delete the shared event, subject to Apple's service handling.
