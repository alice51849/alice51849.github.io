Questions, answered

Is this really free?

Yes — sharpening and enhancing photos here is completely free, with no account and no watermark.

Do you upload my photos?

Never. All processing happens locally in your browser with HTML Canvas. Your images don't leave your device, so it's fully private.

Can it fix a very blurry or out-of-focus image?

This tool recovers mild to moderate blur by sharpening edges and boosting local detail. For heavy motion blur or true AI upscaling, the Unblurry app uses on-device AI for stronger results.

Which formats work?

JPG, PNG and WebP. The download is saved as a high-quality PNG.
